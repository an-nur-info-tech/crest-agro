Environmental and Social Governance (ESG)

Crest Agro Products (CAP) Management team is fully committed to carrying out its business operations as a responsible corporate institution; minimising potential Environmental and Social (E&S) risk impacts while taking advantage of arising opportunities. In line with this, an Environmental and Social Management System (ESMS) has been set up across to CAP to manage Environmental and Social Affairs throughout our operations. We strive to achieve Environmental and Social Governance (ESG) objectives that include:

  • An individual farmer with a minimum of three years’ experience in cassava farming on at least 20 hectares of land in a management or supervisory capacity.
  • An entrepreneur with a minimum of three years’ experience in the management of a farm or a real sector business; or
  • A financial investor that has a team (made up of individuals with at least three years’ experience in cassava farming operations) with required technical expertise to manage the operations at the target scale.
  • A cooperative comprising of smallholder farmers and have at least 5 years experience in farming.

We affirm our commitment to safety, health, environmental and social excellence, to all our stakeholders, including our employees, customers, shareholders, and the public. we will work collaboratively to achieve sustainable business operations. Our efforts to achieve good Environmental and social (E&S) performance are benchmarked against local and international requirements including:

  • International Finance Corporation (IFC) performance standards (PS).
  • African Development Bank (AfDB) environmental and social operational safeguards.
  • World Bank Group Specific (EHS) Guidelines.
  • AFederal Ministry of Environment Regulatory Frame Work.
  • National Environmental Standards and Regulations Enforcement Agency (NESREA).
  • Kogi State Environmental Protection Board Requirements.
  • Kogi State Sanitation and Waste Management Board.
